Performance

You might need to evaluate the application's performance and database as you create and manage apps. The number of open database connections, hardware accessibility, and database access patterns are frequently factors in performance degradation.

Scalability

A database's capacity to continuously modify its resources to satisfy application demands is known as scalability. We offer various elastic, horizontal, and vertical scalability options on premises and on multi-cloud database platforms.

Availability

Through our database solutions, we ensure availability of the database round the clock. The secondary will choose a new primary to become available when the primary goes down or becomes unavailable. The database auto failover and auto switchover will be handled by our solutions with zero data loss.

Distributor Management System

DMS simplifies your sales circle while speeding up the final product delivery. Apart from order management, it also helps distributors to generate their MIS reports and statutory compliance-related reporting. Being an online integrated module, it offers real-time inventory management for distributors enabling them to effortlessly avoid stock-outs, excess supplies and control trade promotion spends. Generating claim statements against the trade promotions is always a tedious activity for distributors. With Featec DMS, the claim generation and settlement process becomes a completely automated process, allowing distributors to use their resources for more important tasks. Our DMS can also be integrated with backend ERP systems, completely automating the process of primary order generation. The system offers flexibility in terms of choosing either the indenting or the replenishment method for primary order management. Additionally, DMS can also be integrated with accounting systems used by distributors, completely eliminating the need to enter sales information for ledger accounting.

Inventory Management

Our inventory management software allows you to categorise your products by name, type, brand, supplier, and supplier code, SKU, to easily build custom reports, perform inventory counts or coordinate with your demand.Our cloud-based inventory management software provides real-time visibility into inventory, with anywhere, anytime access to critical information.

Order Management

Streamline, automate and accelerate collection and processing of orders from different channels. WIth this feature of DMS you can reduce order errors, costs, increase visibility and productivity customer service.

Returns Management

The returns and replacements management system lists all of the items on the order. As the user specified that an item has been returned, they will be asked for a return reason. Depending on the reasons to return, the system will intelligently determine what needs to be done

Scheme Management

You can feed the scheme parameters like scheme concept, validity period, prizes involved, terms and conditions, etc. in the system and disseminate the information across.

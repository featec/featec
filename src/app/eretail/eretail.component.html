E Retail

Online businesses can thrive when they have features like easy payment processing, customer support, and different ways to accept payments online, on the websites and applications. Leverage our E retail application, a custom e-commerce platform to help businesses of all sizes with order management, CRM, shipping, retail analytics, and reporting on one centralised system. This comprehensive solution streamlines processes for managing product information, promotions, pricing, shipping, cancellations, reporting, and more. Powered with advanced analytics, the system enables efficient operations and effective decision-making. The robust modules of the tailor-made solution include.

Product listing & Advanced Search

Aids the customer to quickly browse through the list and do an advanced search of a specific item to find the exact product that is needed in a few clicks.

Realtime Order placement to Nearest Distributor

Helps you to identify the nearest distributor where you can place the order and revoke the inventory in a lesser time cost.

Return Management

offers a centralised panel to manage account reconciliation for marketplaces returns and inventory against all the returns. The return module is highly efficient to manage returns effectively ensuring smooth and quick turnaround time.

Wallet

E-retail wallets are digital wallets that allow you to store your payment information and make secure online payments. They are becoming increasingly popular as a way to make online shopping easier and more convenient.
